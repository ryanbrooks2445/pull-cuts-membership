FAQ

Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Manage your subscription through the Stripe billing portal. You can cancel before your next billing cycle — no long-term contracts.

How fast do you respond?

Most swing reviews are returned within 24–48 hours. Jamie Tier members with live sessions get priority scheduling for weekly calls.

Who is this for — beginners or competitive players?

Both. The pull-cut philosophy scales from players learning ball-flight basics to competitive golfers who need a trusted shot shape under pressure.

What’s the difference between Membership and Jamie Tier?

Membership ($139/mo) includes unlimited async video analysis and drills via Instagram or email. Jamie Tier ($939/mo) adds two live 1-on-1 sessions per week on top of everything in Membership.
